About the Imus City Chamber of Commerce

The Imus City Chamber of Commerce (ICCC) is a non-stock, non-profit, non-government business organization comprised of small, medium, and large enterprises, local chambers and industry associations representing various sectors of business, all working together to foster a healthier economy and improve the viability of business in the community.

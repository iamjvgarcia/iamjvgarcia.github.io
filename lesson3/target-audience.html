Target Audience

Weather watch is designed for people on the go, especially those who work outside their homes in the CALABARZON area. The demographic will be people ages 18 to 50 years old. These are people who constantly travel and need to check weather updates.

Who: Field workers, travelers, and commuters

Age: 18-50

Technology: Smartphones, tablets or iPads, and laptops

Income: ₱ 150,000 to ₱ 300,000
